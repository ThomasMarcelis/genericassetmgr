About the Central Contract Portal

The Central Contract Portal (CCP) is a unified platform designed to streamline message management and inter-team communication across our organization. CMP serves as a single source of truth for all message-related information, making your tasks simpler and more efficient.

What You Can Do

  • Search & Browse: Quickly find messages, view their schemas, related applications, and other details.
  • Register New Messages: Easily add new messages and their schemas to the repository.
  • Manage Message & Application Combinations: Set unique constraints for specific message and application combinations, defining how messages should be tailored to meet specific application needs.
  • Ownership Identification: Have clear visibility of message ownership and decision-making authority.
  • Edit & Manage: Keep your message definitions and constraints up-to-date with an easy-to-use interface.
